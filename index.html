About Us

We are a multidisciplinary team of medical professionals, researchers, and developers from Universiti Sains Islam Malaysia (USIM) dedicated to revolutionizing healthcare through artificial intelligence. Our team combines expertise in medicine, computer science, and linguistics to create innovative solutions that address real-world challenges.

Our mission is to develop AI-powered tools that enhance medical diagnostics, improve patient care, and break down language barriers in healthcare and cultural communication. From disease detection systems to language translation tools, we leverage cutting-edge technology to make healthcare and information more accessible to everyone.

Through collaboration and innovation, we strive to bridge the gap between traditional medical practices and modern technology, creating intelligent solutions that serve communities and advance the field of medical AI.
